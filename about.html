About Cheerly Live Up

We're on a mission to make mental health support accessible to every young person in Africa

🌍 Our Story

Cheerly Live Up was born from a simple observation: too many young people across Africa are struggling with mental health challenges, but accessing support remains difficult due to stigma, cost, and limited availability of services.

We believe that every young person deserves to feel heard, supported, and empowered to navigate life's challenges. That's why we created a wellness companion that's available 24/7, completely private, and designed specifically for African youth.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide free, accessible, and culturally-relevant mental health support to young people across Africa, breaking down barriers and reducing stigma around mental wellbeing.

✨ Our Vision

A future where every young African person has the tools, support, and confidence to prioritize their mental health and live their best life—without shame, without barriers, and with hope.

👥 Meet the Team

Cheerly Live Up is powered by a passionate team of mental health advocates, technologists, and young people who believe in the power of accessible mental health support. We combine expertise in psychology, technology, and African youth culture to create a platform that truly understands and serves our community.

Our diverse team includes mental health professionals, software engineers, cultural consultants, and youth advisors who ensure that every aspect of Cheerly Live Up is built with empathy, cultural sensitivity, and effectiveness.
