💡 Quick Tips

  • Breathe through your nose if you can — it filters and warms the air
  • Sit up straight but don't force it. Relaxed posture helps your lungs open up
  • If you feel dizzy, stop and breathe normally for a bit. No need to push through
  • Try doing this first thing in the morning or right before bed
  • Even 2-3 minutes makes a difference. Consistency beats long sessions
