Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I get started with Cheerly Live Up?
Simply visit our Chat page and start talking! No signup required for basic chat. You can create an account later to save your mood history and preferences.
❓ Is Cheerly Live Up really free?
Yes! Our core services (AI chat support, mood tracking, and resources) are completely free. We believe mental health support should be accessible to everyone.
🔒 Are my conversations private?
Absolutely. Your conversations are confidential and encrypted. We will never share your data without consent, except in rare cases of immediate danger. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
❓ Is this a real therapist or AI?
Cheerly is an AI-powered companion designed to provide supportive conversations and mental health resources. While not a replacement for professional therapy, we offer 24/7 support and can help you find professional help when needed.
❓ What if I'm in crisis?
If you're in immediate danger, please call emergency services (112/911) or visit our Crisis Resources page for helplines and professional support available 24/7.
📱 Do you have a mobile app?
We're currently web-based and work great on mobile browsers! A dedicated mobile app is in our roadmap. For now, you can add our website to your phone's home screen for app-like access.
💬 How quickly will I get a response to my message?
AI chat responses are instant! For email inquiries sent through our contact form, we typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.
🌍 What languages do you support?
Currently, we primarily support English. We're working on adding more African languages to better serve our diverse community. Stay tuned for updates!
